Why Zenith

Built different.
Works consistently.

Zenith Macros is designed for one thing — reliable, clean input automation that doesn't get in your way.

Fully External
Runs outside Minecraft entirely. No memory injection, no packet manipulation, no hooks — clean OS-level input automation that never touches the game process.
Focus Lock
Macros pause the instant Minecraft loses window focus and resume exactly when you return. No accidental input in other windows, no background activation.
Precise Timing Engine
Every macro runs on a tight scheduling loop. Delays are configurable per module and tested to be stable under load — no drift, no missed inputs.
Triggerbot
Pixel-based crosshair detection with Normal, Smart Crit, and S-Tap modes. Fires only when your cursor is on target — controllable, repeatable, consistent.
Macro Profiles
Create and switch between full configurations instantly. Different servers, playstyles, or keybind setups — load a profile and you're ready.
Chat Pause
Macros automatically suspend while the chat window is open so they never interfere with normal typing or command input during play.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is Zenith Macros?
Zenith Macros is an external Minecraft PvP macro client for Windows. It runs as a separate process alongside Minecraft and automates keyboard and mouse inputs for combat sequences. It never injects into the game, reads memory, or modifies packets.
What does "fully external" mean?
External means the client runs outside the Minecraft process entirely. It doesn't hook into the JVM, read game memory, or send fake packets — it only sends inputs to the operating system exactly as a keyboard or mouse would. This is a fundamentally different approach from injected or fabric-based mods.
How does Discord login work?
Click "Login with Discord" and authorize the Zenith Macros application in Discord's OAuth2 flow. We only read your Discord username and ID — we never have access to your messages, servers, or password. Your account on Zenith Macros is tied to your Discord ID.
How do licenses work?
After a successful payment your license key is generated and linked to your Discord account. You can view and copy it from the dashboard at any time. Enter the key in the Zenith Macros client when you first launch it to activate your access.
What is HWID binding?
HWID (hardware ID) binding ties your license to a specific machine when you first activate it. This helps prevent key sharing. If you switch computers or reinstall Windows, you can reset your HWID from the dashboard to re-bind your license to a new machine.
What is the difference between Monthly and Lifetime?
All plans include full access to all modules, all categories, and the dashboard. Monthly is $5/mo (cancel anytime). 3-Month is $10 one-time for 90 days. Lifetime is a $25 one-time payment with no further charges. If you plan to use Zenith Macros long-term, Lifetime is clearly better value.
How do referral codes work?
Create an affiliate profile from the Affiliate tab in your dashboard. You'll get a unique referral code. Share your link as zenithmacros.store/?rreferal=yourcode — anyone who visits and purchases will trigger your commission. You can also apply codes manually at checkout.
Is there a refund policy?
Please read our full Refund Policy for details. If you have issues with your purchase, open a ticket in our Discord server and the support team will assist you directly.
How do I get support?
Join our Discord server and open a support ticket. Our team handles license issues, HWID resets, billing questions, and any technical problems. Response times are generally fast during active hours.
